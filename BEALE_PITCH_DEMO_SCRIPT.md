# 5-Minute Beale Pitch Demo Script

## Demo Setup (30 seconds prep)
- Open Beale web interface: http://localhost:3000
- Have phone ready for SMS demo
- Prepare key scenarios to demonstrate

## Pitch Structure (5 minutes total)

### Opening (30 seconds)
"Imagine you're a busy Memphian who just hit a pothole while driving to work. What do you do? With Beale, our AI assistant, you can get help instantly and safely."

### Demo 1: Resident Experience (1 minute 30 seconds)
**Show the web interface**
1. Open Beale web chat
2. Type: "How do I report a pothole?"
3. Show instant response with step-by-step instructions
4. Click the direct link to demonstrate seamless navigation
5. "See how Beale gives instant answers with direct links - no searching required!"

**SMS Demo**
1. Text to Beale number: "When is trash pickup in Midtown?"
2. Show phone receiving instant response
3. "Works on SMS too - perfect for when you're driving or on mobile!"

### Demo 2: Multilingual Support (45 seconds)
**Spanish Demo**
1. Type: "¿Dónde puedo reportar un bache?" (Where can I report a pothole?)
2. Show instant Spanish response
3. "Beale responds naturally in Spanish, Arabic, and English - breaking language barriers."

### Demo 3: Staff Efficiency (1 minute)
**Show staff perspective**
1. Open a second browser showing "staff monitoring view" (if available)
2. Demonstrate how routine questions are handled automatically
3. "While Beale handles basic questions, staff focus on complex issues like permit disputes or emergency services."

### Impact & Close (1 minute 15 seconds)
**Show metrics**
- "30 seconds vs 15 minutes response time"
- "40% reduction in routine 311 calls"
- "2,000+ hours saved annually for residents"

**Future vision**
- "Soon: mobile app, voice responses, proactive notifications"

**Call to action**
"Beale makes Memphis city services accessible to everyone, everywhere, instantly. Let's make Memphis the most connected city in America!"

---

## Demo Tips
- **Timing**: Practice to hit exactly 5 minutes
- **Backup**: Have screenshots ready if live demo fails
- **Phone**: Use a separate phone for SMS demo
- **Internet**: Ensure stable connection
- **Questions**: Prepare for "How does it work?" and "What's the cost?"

## Key Demo Scenarios
1. **Pothole reporting** - Shows instant access while driving
2. **Trash schedule** - Common routine question
3. **Spanish query** - Demonstrates multilingual support
4. **Permit question** - Shows direct linking to forms

## Backup Plan
If live demo fails:
- Show pre-recorded video (30 seconds)
- Use screenshots with voiceover
- Focus on user stories and metrics

## Practice Script Timing
- Opening: 0:00-0:30
- Web demo: 0:30-1:30
- SMS demo: 1:30-2:00
- Multilingual: 2:00-2:45
- Staff view: 2:45-3:45
- Impact/Close: 3:45-5:00
