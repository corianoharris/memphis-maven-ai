# Resident Use Cases for Beale

## Overview
Beale is an AI-powered city services assistant designed to help Memphis residents access city services quickly and easily. Below are detailed use cases covering all features available to residents.

## Core Features for Residents

### 1. Instant Query Resolution
**Use Case:** A resident needs immediate information about city services
- **Scenario:** Adam is driving and hits a pothole, needs to report it quickly
- **Interaction:** Text or voice message to Beale: "How do I report a pothole?"
- **Response:** Step-by-step instructions with direct links to reporting forms
- **Time Saved:** 28 minutes vs traditional 311 call
- **Features Used:** AI intelligence, semantic search, direct resource linking

### 2. Multilingual Support
**Use Case:** Non-English speaking residents access services in their language
- **Scenario:** Maria needs garbage collection schedule but speaks limited English
- **Interaction:** Text in Spanish: "¿Cuál es el horario de recolección de basura?"
- **Response:** Complete response in Spanish with accurate schedule information
- **Features Used:** Multilingual AI (English, Spanish, Arabic), localized content
- **Impact:** Eliminates language barriers, builds confidence

### 3. Mobile-First Experience
**Use Case:** Residents access services on-the-go via mobile devices
- **Scenario:** James needs business license information while out and about
- **Interaction:** SMS or web interface on mobile device
- **Response:** Mobile-optimized responses with direct links and contact info
- **Features Used:** SMS integration, responsive web interface, voice input
- **Statistics:** 80% of queries from mobile devices

### 4. 24/7 Availability
**Use Case:** Residents need help outside business hours
- **Scenario:** Emergency service question at 2 AM
- **Interaction:** Text message anytime
- **Response:** Instant AI-powered response with relevant information
- **Features Used:** Always-on SMS service, AI availability
- **Impact:** No more waiting for business hours

### 5. Voice Input for Hands-Free Use
**Use Case:** Residents need hands-free interaction while driving or multitasking
- **Scenario:** Driving and need to ask about parking restrictions
- **Interaction:** Voice message: "What's the parking situation downtown?"
- **Response:** Voice or text response with complete information
- **Features Used:** Voice recognition, natural language processing

### 6. Direct Resource Linking
**Use Case:** Residents need specific forms or contact information
- **Scenario:** Need to apply for a permit
- **Interaction:** "How do I get a building permit?"
- **Response:** Direct link to permit application, required documents, contact numbers
- **Features Used:** Semantic search, resource linking, step-by-step guidance

### 7. Memphis Personality and Cultural Relevance
**Use Case:** Residents want engaging, culturally relevant interactions
- **Scenario:** General inquiry about city services
- **Interaction:** Any question about Memphis services
- **Response:** Enthusiastic, Memphis-themed response with local references
- **Features Used:** AI personality training, Memphis-focused knowledge base
